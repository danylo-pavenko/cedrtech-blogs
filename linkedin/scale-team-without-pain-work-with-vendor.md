# LinkedIn post (~150 words)

**Scale Your Team Without the Pain: Working With the Right Vendor**

You need more hands. The roadmap is growing, deadlines are real, and hiring takes months you don't have. So you look at vendors. Outstaffing, dedicated teams, flexible capacity. The idea is simple: get output without the recruitment drama. In practice it often goes wrong. Mismatched expectations, slow ramp-up, communication gaps.

I've worked both sides: scaling vendor relationships for startups and running delivery for teams that slot into someone else's product. What actually works: a single accountable contact, no long ramp (real output in the first two weeks), communication that fits your rituals, and flexibility to pause or scale down without drama. Avoid partners that sell headcount instead of outcomes, black-box delivery, or one-size-fits-all process. Start small, prove the collaboration, then scale. Treat them like team, not "the vendor." The right partner gives you capacity without the pain.
