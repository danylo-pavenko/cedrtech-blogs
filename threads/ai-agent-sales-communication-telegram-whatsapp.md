🧵 **Thread: Turn chat into booking (end-to-end)**

1️⃣ Happy Friday, Serious Growth: while your team is closing tickets, Telegram/WhatsApp leads are asking the same thing: availability, price, and “can we book tomorrow?”. Slow replies create communication debt you can’t refactor later.

2️⃣ The fix is an AI sales agent that executes: answers typical questions, qualifies intent (service + time + constraints), recommends the best option, and triggers booking in your scheduling system. Complex cases get a human handoff with full context.

3️⃣ Engineering mindset = product surface: define “booking-ready conversation” as acceptance criteria, instrument KPIs (reply time, qualified intent, bookings, no-shows, drop-off reasons), and iterate scenarios weekly. End-to-end delivery: [Sales AI](https://sales-ai.com.ua/).
